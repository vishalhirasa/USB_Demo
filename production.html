Key Features of
MPanel Production

  • Powerful smoothing and conversion tools to clean up line models captured via digital
    measuring systems
  • Multiple methods to simplify adding meshes
  • Arrange and convert all meshes to fabric panels with single button
  • Compensation all panels with single button
  • Seam and hem all panels with single button
  • Output to multiple formats for plotter/cutter or hand cutting.

Work Flow

1
Define or clean/smooth digitally capture boundary fixing points/frame
2
Add mesh(es)
3
Form finding (relaxation)
4
Convert mesh(es) to panels
5
Compensate panels
6
Add seams and hems (multiple panels simultaneously)
7
Stamp panel numbers and details
8
Output to file(for plotting), XY coordinates, skeleton
